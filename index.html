OUR PROGRAM

Our 8-month program is divided into14 modules. After the program, students join a 4-week intensive bootcamp to work on a real project with a real partner. Our training takes place at BeCentral(Brussels) everySunday. During the week,students are supported online in their self-study,homework and peer learning.

Our impact

We graduated over 80 people in Belgium and more than 200 globally. In Belgium, over 70% found and retained a high-quality job as a developer.

Our core values

Community

We’re largely dependent on volunteers. That’s what makes us an engaged grassroots community as our students call it 'the HYF family.'

Diversity & inclusiveness

Our program is adapted to suit people with different backgrounds, often in vulnerable situations. We teach on Sundays to allow everyone to take control of their personal lives during the week.

Commitment

Our program is free of charge,although we ask for strong commitment during our 7 month journey. And you know what? Many alumni stay actively involved!

Open

Sharing is caring.Our curriculum is 100% open. We believe in knowledge for many,not the few.
